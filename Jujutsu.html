Jujutsu Kaisen

Jujutsu Kaisen is one of my favorite and first animes. I didnt like to watch anime back then, I only got curious because of my uncle and cousin who are hardcore fans of animes like my hero acadamia. Jujutsu was one of the animes they recommended and I liked it because of how goofy it was and because of the fighting scenes.

My favorite character is Gojo Satoru because he is hilarious and powerful at the same time.
